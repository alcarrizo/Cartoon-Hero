About Jevil

Jevil is a fun character he's the bonus hidden boss in deltarune chapter 1 and as you can imagine, he's one of the hardest bosses in the game.

Jevil's Characteristics

  1. Chaotic
  2. Aware of the fourth wall
  3. Was originally the court jester
  4. Based on the Joker playing card
  5. He can do anything

Jevil's Friend

Due to his imprisonment, he hasn't socialized much, however if we look to before that his best friend would have to be Seam, the form court magician and current shopkeeper
